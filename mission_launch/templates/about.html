🚀 Welcome to Ray’s Space Station

Ray’s Space Station is a Raspberry Pi-powered launch control platform that brings STEM learning to life with rockets, sensors, cameras, and real mission data. It’s not just a launcher — it’s a space education playground.

🧠 What It Teaches

  • How rocket mass, engine size, and drag impact altitude
  • The effects of elevation and humidity on launches
  • How to estimate parachute sizes based on payload
  • Scientific thinking and hands-on experimentation

🛠️ Current Features

  • Live dashboard with countdown, safety toggle, and camera feed
  • Launch logging and event tracking to CSV
  • Manual and automatic abort systems
  • Onboard recorder for altitude and flight time

🚧 Coming Soon

  • Altitude predictor based on motor and weight
  • Graph viewer for launch history by engine type
  • Printable rocket designs and payload kits
  • Community mission sharing and comparison tools
